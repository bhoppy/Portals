New — Portal Login Confirmation

Portal login confirmation SMS — fires immediately when a member completes their first portal login. This is a new touchpoint added from Carl's analysis. Carl's data shows portal login is the single most important early-journey conversion event — all TU-enrolled members were portal-logged-in, none of the never-logged-in members were TU-enrolled. This SMS does two things: (1) confirms the login succeeded and the account is active, (2) reinforces the first debit date and amount before the member leaves the session. It also sets the expectation for the pre-debit reminder ("you'll get a reminder 3 days before"), which reduces surprise and support contacts. Fires once per member, suppressed after first send.
